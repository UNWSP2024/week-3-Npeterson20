Steps of the flowchart:

1. Start
2. Display menu for hot dog selection:
3. Hot Dog ($3.50)
4. Chili Dog ($4.50)
5. Input hot dog choice (set variable dog_cost)
6. Display menu for optional toppings:
7. Cheese ($0.50)
8. Peppers ($0.75)
9. Grilled Onions ($1.00)
10. Input the chosen toppings and add their prices to the total (set variable topping_cost)
11. Calculate the subtotal (subtotal = dog_cost + topping_cost)
12. Calculate tax (7% of the subtotal)
13. Calculate the total cost (total = subtotal + tax)
14. Display the hot dog cost, tax, and total cost
End
